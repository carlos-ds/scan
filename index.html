Considerations

When scanning, be sure to take the following considerations (freely copied from the Instascan readme) into account:

  • Keep barcodes as horizontal as possible when scanning.
  • Scan in an environment with normal light conditions. Avoid direct sunlight or overly dark environments.
  • Cameras with auto-focus make detection more difficult.
